BUTTER CROISSANT

There are not many dishes and products that have gained such extreme popularity as the French butter croissant. Anyone who has tried it at least once will remember this delicate creamy taste and delicate texture of the dough forever.

The technology of making real croissants is quite complex and time-consuming, and consists of many important details, each of which is a brick of product quality. If your establishment does not specialize in baking, it is quite difficult to provide fresh, high-quality croissants for your guests on your own.
